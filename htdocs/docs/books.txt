%(title)s
=========

As of August 2009, there are three book on CouchDB being written and due to be
 published in late 2009 or early 2010:

 * [CouchDB: The Definitive Guide][relax], O'Reilly Media, by J Chris Anderson, 
   Jan Lehnardt &amp; Noah Slater
 * [CouchDB in Action][inaction], Manning, by Chris Chandler
 * [Beginning CouchDB][beginning], Apress, by Joe Lennon

[relax]: http://books.couchdb.org/relax
[inaction]: http://manning.com/chandler/
[beginning]: http://www.apress.com/book/view/9781430272373
